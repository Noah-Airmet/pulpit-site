Conference record · 1843 April General Conference · 1843-04-06

Preferred witness is the Joseph Smith Papers transcript of JS History, volume D-1. The record preserves conference minutes, business actions, and reported discourse summaries from the April 1843 conference; it should not be treated as verbatim transcripts of discrete talks.

Moving and Purification

Heber C. Kimball, First Counselor in the First Presidency

1858 April General Conference·Afternoon Session·1858-04-06

Brief remarks by President Heber C. Kimball at the April 1858 General Conference, delivered during the Utah War "Move South." Reported in conference minutes. ¶

Kimball shared his personal relocation efforts and spoke of the spiritual benefits of the current move southward. ¶

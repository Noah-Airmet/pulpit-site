Minutes and Notes of the October 1845 Nauvoo General Conference

Conference record · 1845 October General Conference · 1845-10-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Handwritten minutes and notes for the October 6-8, 1845 Nauvoo General Conference. Bullock/Bolton is the preferred base witness for October 6-7; the Thomas Bullock booklet picks up with October 8 afternoon conference material; William Clayton is a full-conference control witness but has document damage and should be collated rather than silently merged. These are minutes and summaries, not verbatim transcripts of discrete talks.

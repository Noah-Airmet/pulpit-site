Opposition to the Work of God, Etc.

John Taylor · 1879 October General Conference · 1879-10-07

Fidelity

◑Contemporaneous report

Published in Journal of Discourses, Volume 21, pp. 373-376, continued in Volume 22, pp. 1-4 (JD's own printing crosses a volume boundary mid-discourse; the printed text itself, and the delivery, are continuous — one address, Tuesday afternoon, Oct. 7, 1879). Reported by Geo. F. Gibbs in Pitman shorthand; the speaker did not review this text before publication. Text transcribed from the printed JD.

Perpetual Emigrating Fund, Etc.

George A. Smith · 1854 October General Conference · 1854-10-06

Fidelity

◑Contemporaneous report

Published in Journal of Discourses, Volume 7, pp. 59-62, delivered October 6, 1854. A prior pass flagged this citation as needing verification, reasoning that JD Vol. 7 mostly covers 1858-1860 discourses and would not normally contain an October 1854 talk — heavyweight review confirmed via jod.mrm.org (used only as a private clue) and independent web search that JD volumes were compiled non-chronologically and Vol. 7 does in fact contain this 1854 discourse at pp. 59-62. Smith's discourse addressed the Perpetual Emigrating Fund and the golden rule, urging newly arrived immigrants to prioritize repaying travel debts before acquiring personal property. Pitman shorthand report; the speaker did not review this text before publication. Full text confirmed complete, opening and closing sentences verified verbatim.

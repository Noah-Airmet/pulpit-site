Minutes of the April 1855 General Conference

Conference record · 1855 April General Conference · 1855-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes (April 11, 1855, p. 36) of the April 6-8, 1855 General Conference, the 25th-anniversary conference. All discourses from this conference that survive in the Journal of Discourses are filed separately as full talks: Brigham Young's 'Faithfulness and Apostasy' and 'Preaching and Testimony' (JD 2:266-271), Orson Pratt's 'Progress of the Work' (JD 2:259-266), Ezra T. Benson's 'The Word of Wisdom' (JD 2:357-359), and George A. Smith's 'Ordaining Young Men to Office' (JD 2:360-368). Corrected during heavyweight review; a prior pass of this note incorrectly claimed all but Faithfulness and Apostasy were not yet transcribed. The many shorter remarks and sustainings across the three-day conference remain open work for a future ingestion pass.

Heavyweight pass: verified against archive.org/details/conferencereport1912a (internal scan conferencereport821chur), leaves 79-83 (page/n79, running head badly OCR-garbled as 'ELDER JOSEPH E. EOBINSOff.'). Confirmed boundaries: begins immediately after Elder John L. Herrick's address and a soprano solo ('The Palms,' Faurier, Sister Irma Pendleton), with '(President of California Mission.) It is indeed gratifying, my brethren and sisters, to meet with those who agree in faith and in doctrine...'; recounts a conversation with a chaplain who doubted Christ's historicity, citing Tacitus, Suetonius, Josephus, and Publius Lentulus, then the Easter resurrection narrative; ends '...that we with the love of the Lord shall shine in the firmament as stars forever. I ask it in the name of Jesus. Amen.', followed by the hymn 'Come, Come, Ye Saints' and then Elder Melvin J. Ballard's address. Two stray running-head bleeds within the body (one an exact repeat of the byline, garbled as 'ELDER JOSEPH E. ROBINSON.', mid-sentence at a leaf transition) and one at the tail ('ELDER MELVIN J. BALLARD.') were excluded as page-proof artifacts, confirmed by extending the range each time until the sentence actually completed. De-hyphenated, reflowed, corrected scattered OCR errors, and removed the printed synopsis block per docs/review.md.

Reconstructed correct reading order by hand from the page image logic: byline+synopsis, then "This morning we have met together...", then "In reading the scriptures, which I trust this great congregation is accustomed to do..." (the two halves of one sentence that the OCR had printed on either side of the scripture quotations). Corrected session/date from "Second Day Morning Session"/1913-10-05 to "Third Day Morning Session"/1913-10-06. Removed a stray running-head bleed mid-body ("ELDER DAVID O. McKAY.", the running head for the NEXT talk printed one page early; confirmed by content, the surrounding paragraphs are continuous with Smith's own talk in the first person).

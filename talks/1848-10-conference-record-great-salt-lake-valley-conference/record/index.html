Minutes of the October 1848 Great Salt Lake Valley General Conference

Conference record · 1848 October General Conference · 1848-10-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Full transcription pass (2026-07-24, claude-fable-5): all 17 leaves of the CHL manuscript (Historian's Office general Church minutes, 1839-1877, '1848 September-December' / 'General Conference minutes, Salt Lake City,' CR 100 318, recorded by Thomas Bullock) are now transcribed below by direct reading of the CHL leaf images, fetched via the catalog's own delivery API (accessLevelCode: unconditional confirmed per leaf before download). Method: four overlapping 2x-scale contrast-enhanced bands per leaf plus targeted 3-4x zooms. The record covers the October 6 opening and the adjourned Sunday sessions of October 8, 15, 22, and 29, 1848 (Bullock's closing docket lists all five dates), plus a separate revised fair copy of Brigham Young's October 8 priesthood address (leaves 14-17, docketed as an epistle 'revised copied & sent' to O. Hyde and G. A. Smith). Two readings in this file's earlier partial transcription are corrected against the scan: the manuscript reads '10 A.M.' (not '10 1/2 A.M.') and '^General Conference' with 'General' interlined by caret (not 'Special Conference'). Leaf 13 is a duplicate oblique capture of leaf 12. Still graded fragmentary because Bullock's minute-hand carries word-level uncertainty in spots; uncertain readings are bracketed in place, cancellations recorded as [struck: ...], interlineations as ^[...].

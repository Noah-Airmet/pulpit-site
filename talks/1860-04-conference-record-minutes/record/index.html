Minutes of the April 1860 General Conference

Conference record · 1860 April General Conference · 1860-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes (April 11, 1860, pp. 45, 48) of the April 6-8, 1860 General Conference, the Church's 30th-anniversary (31st Annual) conference. All five discourses named in these minutes are filed separately as full talks: Brigham Young's 'Universal Salvation' (JD 8:34-36), 'Personal Reminiscences, &c' (JD 8:37-39), 'Voting to Sustain the Authorities of the Church—Appointment of Elder Cannon to Fill Up the Quorum of the Twelve—Remarks to Departing Missionaries' (JD 7:227-230), and 'Education—Testimony—Miraculous Signs, &c' (JD 8:39-43), and Orson Pratt's 'Testimony of the Spirit, Etc.' (JD 8:44-52).

Smith, Jr., unanimous vote), then the printed synopsis 'Descendants of righteous men admonished to be faithful.—Saints should diligently obey God's laws.' and 'I appreciate the kindness of my brother, Elder Herrick, in proffering me the time that was alloted to him...'; ends '...I pray that we may all be faithful and true in the discharge of every duty as Latter-day Saints, in the name of Jesus Christ. Amen.', followed by the anthem 'Inflammatus' and benediction by President Hugh J. Cannon, closing the Third Day Second Overflow Meeting. A separate, concurrent Third Day meeting ('Barratt Hall Meeting,' presided over by President Seymour B. Young) follows as a new session with Joseph Eckersley's address.

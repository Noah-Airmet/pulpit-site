Testimony of the Spirit—Revelation Given According to Requirements—Spiritual Warfare and Conquest, Etc.

Brigham Young · 1857 October General Conference · 1857-10-07

Fidelity

◑Contemporaneous report

Published in Journal of Discourses, Volume 5, pp. 327-333 (delivered October 7, 1857, Wednesday afternoon). This is Young's second discourse at the October 1857 conference; his first ('Advantages of Trials and Experience,' JD 5:293-296, October 6) is filed separately. Conference held while Johnston's Army was marching toward Utah Territory. Text below is taken from the raw HTML of the historicalgeneralconferences.weebly.com transcript (fetched directly to avoid AI-summarization truncation), cross-checked against jod.mrm.org as a private clue. Pitman shorthand report by G. D. Watt and J. V. Long; the speaker did not review this text before publication.

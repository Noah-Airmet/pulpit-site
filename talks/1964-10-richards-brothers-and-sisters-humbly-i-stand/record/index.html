Brothers And Sisters, Humbly I Stand...

LeGrand Richards · 1964 October General Conference · 1964-10-03

Backfill of a talk flagged as a known gap in progress/conference-reports.md (1964 October DATA QUALITY FLAG: 'LeGrand Richards' address was never ingested into the vault'). Located via the report's own back-of-book index and session headers, and confirmed by President N. Eldon Tanner's own transition remarks introducing Richards after Elder Delbert L. Stapley and before Elder S. Dilworth Young (leaf n66-69 of conferencereport1964sa). OCR ingestion with deterministic cleanup: de-hyphenated line-break splits, removed running heads/page numbers/leaf markers, reflowed paragraphs, kept the printed editorial side-headings as ## headings. Fixed two OCR character errors ('Mai. 3:7' -> 'Mal. 3:7' and 'then-lives' -> 'their lives'); both are unambiguous OCR misreads corrected against the surrounding text and the King James citation, not paraphrasing. The closing paragraph on leaf n69 carries a running-head bleed reading 'ELDER S. DILWORTH YOUNG' even though the paragraph is still Richards's own words (the printed running head names the speaker introduced later on that page, a known pattern in this era) — confirmed by President Tanner's remarks immediately following, which state Richards 'has just spoken' before introducing Young. talk_order corrected: Stapley remains 1, Richards inserted as 2, S. Dilworth Young's file bumped from 2 to 3 to reflect the true speaking sequence. Archive snapshot pending.

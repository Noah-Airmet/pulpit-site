Transcribed from the OCR of the official printed Conference Report published by the Deseret News and hosted at the Internet Archive (conferencereport1926sa). This file is a direct mid-sentence continuation of President Grant's officer-changes report from 1926-10-01 (no new speaker heading in the source) through his reading of Alma 29 and remarks on the joy of missionary work; it continues without a break into 1926-10-03. Running heads, page numbers, and line-break hyphenation removed.

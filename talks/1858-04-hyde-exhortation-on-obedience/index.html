Exhortation on Obedience

Orson Hyde, President of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles

1858 April General Conference·Morning Session·1858-04-06

Brief morning remarks by Elder Orson Hyde at the April 1858 General Conference, delivered during the Utah War. Reported in conference minutes. ¶

Hyde encouraged the Saints to follow God's requirements, noting that present circumstances would "purify the Saints." ¶

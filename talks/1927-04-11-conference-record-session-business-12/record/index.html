Closing of the First Day Afternoon Meeting and Opening of the First Overflow Meeting

Conference record · 1927 April General Conference · 1927-04-03

Fidelity

◕Authoritative print

Session housekeeping (choir anthem, brief remarks by President Grant on conference announcements, prayer, adjournment) and the opening record of the First Overflow Meeting (presiding officer, music, hymn, prayer) transcribed from the OCR of the official printed Conference Report published by the Deseret News and hosted at the Internet Archive (conferencereport1927a). Running heads and page numbers removed.

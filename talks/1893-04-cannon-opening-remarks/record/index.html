Heavyweight pass: fetched and read the full Deseret Weekly proceedings for this conference (Apr 8, 1893 issue, pp. 493-498, and Apr 15, 1893 issue, pp. 513-516), directly. This is the Salt Lake Temple dedication conference: the regular business session ran only Tuesday Apr 4 - Wednesday Apr 5 (Woodruff cut it short Wednesday afternoon so the congregation could prepare for the dedication), with Thursday Apr 6 onward being the separate 31-session Temple dedication itself.

Remarks on Meetinghouse Sunday School Facilities

Joseph M. Tanner · 1903 April General Conference · 1903-04-06

Fidelity

◕Authoritative print

Transcribed from the Church History Library scan of the official printed Conference Report of the Seventy-Third Annual Conference (April 1903), Conference Report (English, 1880-2017) Volume 7, catalog record db680f1b-d7fa-46b5-9252-143bcc36d460, printed p. 82 (image leaf = printed page + 1, confirmed against the printed page number visible on the leaf). This closing remarks talk immediately follows Joseph F. Smith's closing remarks at the same Deseret Sunday School Union Meeting ([[1903-04-smith-sunday-school-remarks]]); no Internet Archive witness exists for this talk since the IA mirror (conferencereport1903a) is missing this leaf entirely (see that talk's provenance notes). OCR corrected by hand (running heads removed, paragraphing restored, obvious OCR errors corrected). Grade reflects the officially published Conference Report. The session-closing lines that follow Tanner's "Amen" in the source (choir number, benediction credit, and the secretary/reporter signature block) are administrative session material, not part of the talk, and are excluded per convention.

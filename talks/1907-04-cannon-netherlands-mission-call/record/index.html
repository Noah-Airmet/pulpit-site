I Rejoice in Being Present at This Conference

Sylvester Q. Cannon · 1907 April General Conference · 1907-04-07

Fidelity

◕Authoritative print

Transcribed from the OCR of the official printed Conference Report of the Seventy-Seventh Annual Conference (April 1907), hosted at the Internet Archive (conferencereport1907a). Running heads and line-break hyphenation removed. The speaker's printed middle initial is genuinely ambiguous in the source scan's OCR layer (renders as a lowercase 'a.'); identified as Sylvester Q. Cannon based on the historical record of a missionary of that name called to labor in the Netherlands/Belgium mission around this period, but the scan's own letterform is not fully legible and is flagged here rather than silently normalized.

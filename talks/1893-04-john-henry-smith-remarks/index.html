Remarks

John Henry Smith, Quorum of the Twelve Apostles

1893 April General Conference·Wednesday Afternoon Session·1893-04-05

Elder John Henry Smith, was the next speaker. He too, touched briefly on the subject of the Temple, and said that at this time many eyes were turned Zionward. To his mind the greatest missionary experience in the history of this Church was taking place today, and he himself counseled the Saints so to live that they would be found walking in the path of eternal life. ¶

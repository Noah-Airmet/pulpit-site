Minutes of the September 1851 Semi-Annual General Conference

Conference record · 1851 September General Conference · 1851-09-07

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

The September 1851 Semi-Annual Conference proceedings survive only as a summary paragraph within the Sixth General Epistle of the First Presidency, published in the Deseret News on November 15, 1851. No individual sermon transcripts are preserved. The conference was scheduled as 'October' in the standard semi-annual pattern but was actually held September 7-10, 1851.

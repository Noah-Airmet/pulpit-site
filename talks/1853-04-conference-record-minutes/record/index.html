Minutes of the April 1853 General Conference

Conference record · 1853 April General Conference · 1853-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the April 6-10, 1853 General Conference, the occasion of the Salt Lake Temple cornerstone-laying ceremony. All four cornerstone orations and prayers are filed separately as full talks: the Southeast (Brigham Young, Heber C. Kimball), Southwest (Edward Hunter, Alfred Cordon), Northwest (John Young, George B. Wallace), and Northeast (Parley P. Pratt, Orson Hyde). Corrected during heavyweight review; a prior pass of this note incorrectly claimed the Northwest and Northeast talks were not yet transcribed, when they already existed as separate files. The later-conference discourses by Orson Hyde ('Sanctification—Economy—Apostates,' JD 1:71-73), John Taylor ('Legitimacy and Illegitimacy,' JD 1:221-233), Parley P. Pratt ('Heirship and Priesthood,' JD 1:256-263), and Brigham Young ('Heirship,' JD 6:306-308; 'The Temple Cornerstones—The Apostleship, &c,' JD 1:131-137) from April 6-10 remain open work for a future ingestion pass.

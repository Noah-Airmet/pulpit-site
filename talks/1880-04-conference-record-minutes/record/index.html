Minutes of the April 1880 General Conference

Conference record · 1880 April General Conference · 1880-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the April 6-8, 1880 General Conference (Fiftieth Annual Conference), with preparatory meetings April 4-5 in the Salt Lake Assembly Hall. President John Taylor presiding. This was the Year of Jubilee — the Church's fiftieth anniversary. The Weebly page for this conference carries no bibliography section and cites no Journal of Discourses volume or page for any speaker at all (reconfirmed 2026-07-01), unlike almost every other conference in this era; no evidence was found that any discourse from this conference was published in JD. This is why zero talk files exist for this conference — not a vault gap awaiting transcription. All named speakers (Taylor, Woodruff, Thatcher, Pratt, Rich, Joseph F. Smith, Wells, Brigham Young Jr., Erastus Snow, Richards, Carrington, Lorenzo Snow) are recorded only as third-person Deseret News summaries.

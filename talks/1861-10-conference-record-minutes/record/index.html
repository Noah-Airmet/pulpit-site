Minutes of the October 1861 General Conference

Conference record · 1861 October General Conference · 1861-10-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes (October 23, 1861, pp. 185-186) of the Semi-Annual Conference, October 6-8, 1861. Includes the dedicatory prayer for the repaired and enlarged Tabernacle, delivered by Brigham Young on the evening of October 6, 1861. Heber C. Kimball's 'Building of the Temple' discourse and George A. Smith's 'Importance of the Mission to the Cotton Country' are filed separately as full talks. Most other remarks (Taylor, Hyde, Wells, Woolley, Phineas H. Young, Samuel W. Richards, George D. Watt, Carrington, Benson, Coombs, Morley, Phelps, Miles, Snow, Pratt) are brief and summarized here rather than transcribed in full, as the source preserves only third-person summaries for them, not verbatim text. Coverage now extends through the full three-day conference (Sunday Oct. 6 through Tuesday Oct. 8 close), not only the first two days.

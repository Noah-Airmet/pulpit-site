Minutes of the April 1857 General Conference

Conference record · 1857 April General Conference · 1857-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News, April 15, 1857, minutes of the April 6-9, 1857 General Conference. Discourses by Brigham Young (two), Daniel H. Wells, Heber C. Kimball, Wilford Woodruff (two), Lorenzo Snow, Joseph Young, and John Young are all filed separately as full talks. Corrected during heavyweight review; a prior pass of this note incorrectly claimed none were transcribed. This conference occurred in the context of growing tension with the U.S. federal government, with President Buchanan's administration preparing the Utah Expedition.

The Saints' Disposition

Lorenzo Snow, Apostle, Quorum of the Twelve

1858 April General Conference·Afternoon Session·1858-04-06

Brief remarks by Elder Lorenzo Snow at the April 1858 General Conference, delivered during the Utah War. Reported in conference minutes. ¶

Snow noted the joyful countenances of the Saints despite the difficult circumstances of the Utah War period. ¶

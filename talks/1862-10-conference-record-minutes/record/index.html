Minutes of the October 1862 General Conference

Conference record · 1862 October General Conference · 1862-10-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes (October 15, 1862, pp. 124-125) of the Semi-Annual Conference, October 6-7, 1862. Brigham Young's 'Never-Ending Character of a Saints' Mission' and 'Future State of Existence' discourses, Heber C. Kimball's 'Our Duties to God Paramount to All Other Obligations—Danger of Speculation' (JD 10:75-78), Charles C. Rich's 'Present Opportunities of Obtaining a Knowledge of the Principles of Truth' (JD 10:90-95), Amasa M. Lyman's 'The Object of Gathering' (JD 10:83-90), Orson Hyde's two discourses (JD 10:70-75, 112-113), and George A. Smith's 'The Importance of the Mission to the Cotton Country' (JD 10:121-122, October 8) are all filed separately as full talks in this vault. This record preserves the surrounding minutes/order-of-business only.

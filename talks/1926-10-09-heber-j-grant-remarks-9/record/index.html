TABERNACLE CHOIR VISITS CALIFORNIA

Heber J. Grant · 1926 October General Conference · 1926-10-03

Fidelity

◕Authoritative print

Transcribed from the OCR of the official printed Conference Report published by the Deseret News and hosted at the Internet Archive (conferencereport1926sa). Running heads, page numbers, and line-break hyphenation removed. This is the closing portion of President Grant's Afternoon Session remarks (Tabernacle Choir report and Peter Whitmer Farm purchase announcement), following directly after Elder Ballard's address with no intervening speaker heading.

Minutes of the April 1856 General Conference

Conference record · 1856 April General Conference · 1856-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the April 6-8, 1856 General Conference. George A. Smith's 'Leaven of the Gospel' discourse (JD 3:280-291), Orson Pratt's 'Advantages of the Latter-day Saints' discourse (JD 3:299-307), and Parley P. Pratt's 'Progress of the Latter-day Church' discourse (JD 3:307-316) are filed separately as full talks. Corrected during heavyweight review; a prior pass of this note did not cross-reference the Parley P. Pratt talk file even though it already existed. Wilford Woodruff's, Lorenzo Snow's, Heber C. Kimball's, and other speakers across the three-day conference are NOT yet transcribed — open for a follow-up pass.

The Leaven of the Gospel—The Saints Should Divest Themselves of Old Traditions—Policy of Making Good Farms and Storing Up Grain

George A. Smith · 1856 April General Conference · 1856-04-06

Fidelity

◑Contemporaneous report

Published in Journal of Discourses, Volume 3, pp. 280-291, delivered April 6, 1856 (confirmed via independent web search and jod.mrm.org, used only as a private clue). The BYU ContentDM viewer does not expose a stable per-page deep link, so the collection landing page is recorded as a witness rather than source_url. Includes the speaker's account of the Thomas B. Marsh 'milk strippings' dispute traditionally linked to Marsh's 1838-39 estrangement and the Missouri extermination order, presented as the speaker's own retelling, not independently verified by this project. Pitman shorthand report by George D. Watt; speaker did not review this text before publication.

Minutes of the October 1856 General Conference

Conference record · 1856 October General Conference · 1856-10-05

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News, October 15, 1856, minutes of the October 5-7, 1856 General Conference. Brigham Young's October 5 pre-conference discourse 'Discord at Meetings Rebuked—A Text for Speakers at the Conference' (JD 4) is filed separately. Wilford Woodruff's October 6 discourse is also filed separately. Lorenzo Snow, C. H. Wheelock, James Ferguson, Patriarch John Young, and other speakers are NOT yet transcribed — open for a follow-up pass. This conference is historically notable for Brigham Young's handcart rescue call.

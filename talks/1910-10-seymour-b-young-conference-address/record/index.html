Reading order was reconstructed from the DjVu word coordinates with scripts/ingest/cr-reading-order.py, segmented with scripts/ingest/cr-segment-legacy.py, then cleaned: running heads, page numbers, and line-break hyphenation removed; paragraphs reflowed; obvious OCR errors partially corrected. The printed report supplies an editorial topic-summary for each discourse, preserved at the top of the body when present; the title is supplied from that summary.

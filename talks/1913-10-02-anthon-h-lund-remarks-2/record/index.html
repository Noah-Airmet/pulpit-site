Collated word-by-word against the original scan.

Heavyweight pass: verified against archive.org/details/conferencereport1913sa leaves 9-13 (page/n9), collated against the page scans. This talk follows immediately after Joseph F. Smith's Opening Address (see 1913-10-01) and is confirmed by its own printed synopsis ("Blessing attending Conference gathings...") and by the "PRESIDENT ANTHON H. LUND." running head on every subsequent page through its close ("...I ask it in the name of Jesus. Amen."). Anthon H. Lund was serving as Second Counselor in the First Presidency at this conference. Confirmed the boundary: Joseph F. Smith's brief follow-up remarks on the Word of Wisdom ("If I may be indulged just a moment...", see 1913-10-03) begin immediately after this talk's closing Amen, under a "PRESIDENT JOSEPH F. SMITH." byline, followed by a closing hymn and the adjournment of the First Day Morning Session.

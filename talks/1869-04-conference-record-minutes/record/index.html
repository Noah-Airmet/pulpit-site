Minutes of the April 1869 General Conference

Conference record · 1869 April General Conference · 1869-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the Fortieth Annual Conference, April 6-8, 1869 (corrected from a prior 'Thirty-Ninth' misnumbering — the Weebly page's own masthead reads 'FORTIETH ANNUAL CONFERENCE'). All 6 discourses with surviving JD full text are filed separately as talks: George A. Smith's 'Contributions for Emigrating the Saints...' and George Q. Cannon's 'The Order of Enoch...' (both Tuesday AM, JD 13); Brigham Young's 'Cooperation' (Tuesday PM, JD 12 — a JD publication-lag quirk, printed in a different volume than the rest of the conference); Daniel H. Wells' 'Cooperation—Merchandising and Productive Businesses...' (Wednesday AM, JD 13); Brigham Young's 'Responsibility for Teachings...' (Wednesday PM, JD 13); and Brigham Young's 'Gathering the Saints...' (Thursday AM, JD 13) — confirmed against the Weebly page's own bibliography, which lists no other JD citations for this conference. The many other named speakers (Ezra T. Benson, Franklin D. Richards, Erastus Snow, Orson Pratt, Wilford Woodruff) and Brigham Young's Thursday-afternoon 'State of the Church' remarks are recorded only as third-person Deseret News summaries, with no full JD text located — consistent with the bibliography, not a vault gap.

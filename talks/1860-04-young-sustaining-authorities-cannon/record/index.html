Voting to Sustain the Authorities of the Church—Appointment of Elder Cannon to Fill Up the Quorum of the Twelve—Remarks to Departing Missionaries

Brigham Young · 1860 April General Conference · 1860-04-07

Fidelity

◑Contemporaneous report

Published in Journal of Discourses, Volume 7, pp. 227-230 (delivered April 7, 1860). This discourse includes Young's announcement of George Q. Cannon's appointment to fill a vacancy in the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles. Pitman shorthand report; the speaker did not review this text before publication. Text transcribed from the printed JD.

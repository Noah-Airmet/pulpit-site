Minutes of the October 1879 General Conference

Conference record · 1879 October General Conference · 1879-10-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the October 6-8, 1879 General Conference (Forty-Ninth Semi-Annual Conference). President John Taylor presiding. All 7 discourses with surviving JD full text are filed separately as talks: Orson Pratt's 'Progress of the Work of God...' and Lorenzo Snow's 'The Church Founded Upon the Rock...' (First Day AM); Franklin D. Richards' 'Preaching of John the Baptist...' and John Taylor's 'The Work of God Cannot Be Hindered...' (First Day PM); John Taylor's 'Opposition to the Work of God, Etc.' (Tuesday October 7 PM — a single discourse JD itself splits across Volumes 21 and 22, merged into one file); George Q. Cannon's 'The Righteous Suffer Persecution...' (Tuesday evening); and Erastus Snow's 'Cleave to Light...' (Wednesday October 8 AM) — confirmed against the Weebly page's own bibliography, which lists no other JD citations for this conference. The many other named speakers (Young Jr., Joseph Young, Joseph F. Smith, Wells, Thatcher, Angus M. Cannon, Zina Young) are recorded only as third-person Deseret News summaries, with no full JD text located — consistent with the bibliography, not a vault gap. Notable: President John Taylor prophesied severe national judgment if persecution of the Saints continued.

More In This Present Time And In...

Eldred G. Smith, Presiding Patriarch

1967 April General Conference·Second Day Afternoon Meeting·1967-04-08

more in this present time, and in the world to come life everlasting." (Luke 18:29-30.) How true this is! ¶

Each of us has his duties to perform, and to perform them faithfully should be our constant aim, even though selfdenial is required. ¶

Let us all appreciate that today as of old: "Sacrifice brings forth the blessings of heaven." ¶

May we each put the Church first in our lives and more fully understand and appreciate the eternal law of sacrifice. May sacrifice become a part of our daily lives, that we and our families may enjoy the blessings derived therefrom, I pray, in the name of Jesus Christ. Amen. ¶

Minutes of the June 1854 Special Conference

Conference record · 1854 June General Conference · 1854-06-27

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes (July 6, 1854, p. 32) of a special conference held in the Tabernacle, Great Salt Lake City, June 27-28, 1854, in addition to the regular April and October conferences of that year. This conference was held to mark the tenth anniversary of the martyrdom of Joseph and Hyrum Smith (Carthage Jail, June 27, 1844); the timing and choice of speakers/topics were deliberately keyed to that anniversary. Of the conference's speakers, only President Jedediah M. Grant's June 28 address ('Apostasy, &c.') was ever republished in full (Journal of Discourses 6:253-254) and is filed separately as 1854-06-grant-apostasy.md. The remarks of President Brigham Young, Elder John Taylor, President Heber C. Kimball, and Elder Lorenzo Snow survive only as the brief third-person summaries recorded below; no verbatim transcript of their remarks at this conference has been located.

Session notes: closing session opening, music and prayer

Conference record · 1911 October General Conference · 1911-10-08

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Session housekeeping notes (hymn, benediction, and the opening of the Closing Session: choir and prayer) misfiled in the original ingest as an individual discourse attributed to "Tamihani Te Awe Awe." Reclassified as record_type: conference_record during this heavyweight pass; no individual sermon content was lost. Tamihani Te Awe Awe is recorded here only as pronouncing the benediction that closed the Outdoor Meeting -- he does not appear elsewhere in this conference as a discourse speaker.

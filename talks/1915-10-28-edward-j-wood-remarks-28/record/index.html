Collated word-by-word against the original scan.

Heavyweight pass: source verified against conferencereport1915sa OCR. Reading-order reconstruction (scripts/ingest/cr-reading-order.py, 140 leaves) showed this whole passage -- from "I come from the extreme north" through the closing "Amen" -- is one continuous address by Edward J. Wood, President of Alberta Stake, in the First Day Afternoon Session (Second Meeting Out-of-Doors). The original ingest had split it into three files: this one (talk_order 28), a fragment misfiled as an unidentified speaker (1915-10-29-unknown-anecdote-brother-parker-book-of-mormon.md), and a fragment misattributed to a speaker named "Parker" (1915-10-30-parker-remarks-30.md) -- but "Brother/Bishop Parker" is a character in Wood's own anecdote about missionary work among the Kree Indians near the Alberta stake, not the person speaking. Merged all three back into this single file verbatim from OCR; talk_order 29 and 30 are now vacant. Session corrected from "First Day Afternoon Session" (Tabernacle) to "Second Meeting Out-of-Doors" per the true session header at OCR line 6440-6441.

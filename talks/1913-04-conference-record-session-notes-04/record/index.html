Session notes: correspondence school notice, choir concert notice, and adjournment

Conference record · 1913 April General Conference · 1913-04-04

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Session housekeeping notes closing the First Day Morning Session and opening the First Day Afternoon Session: a notice read by President Joseph F. Smith about the Church's correspondence/missionary classes, a notice about a choir concert, closing hymn and benediction, adjournment, then the afternoon session's opening hymn and prayer. Reclassified as record_type: conference_record; no individual sermon content was lost or removed.

M204.1 A1 1880, 1897-). This is the weekly 'Music and the Spoken Word' broadcast from the Salt Lake Tabernacle Choir and Organ, written and announced by Richard L. Evans and carried by CBS Radio, presented 9:30-10:00 a.m. Sunday, April 8, 1956 (the conference's Third Day) -- the 1390th installment of this long-running broadcast, per its own closing announcement. Split out of 1956-04-smith-at-this-season-of-the-year-the.md, where a prior lightweight pass had left this entire broadcast conflated onto the back of Elder Joseph Fielding Smith's separate Church of the Air address (a distinct, same-day broadcast) rather than losing the content in a light-touch pass. Extracted only Evans's own spoken-word essay text (on the immortality and eternal value of learning and intelligence), leaving out the hymn/organ-selection announcer script and choir cues as non-talk broadcast material, consistent with how this vault treats session music elsewhere. Page-anchored source_url set to leaf n128 (where the broadcast script proper begins, following the 'CHOIR AND ORGAN BROADCAST' heading); the essay text itself runs to leaf n129.

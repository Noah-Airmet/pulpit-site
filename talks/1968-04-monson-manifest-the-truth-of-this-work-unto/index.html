Manifest The Truth Of This Work Unto...

Thomas S. Monson, Quorum of the Twelve Apostles

1968 April General Conference·Third Day Morning Meeting·1968-04-07

manifest the truth of this work unto them other than by hearsay, just as when Jesus said to his disciples: ¶

"Whom do men say that I the Son of man am? ¶

"And they said, some say that thou art . . . one of the prophets. ¶

". . . But whom say ye that I am?" ¶

And Peter answered, "Thou art the Christ, the Son of the living God." ¶

And then Jesus said, "Blessed art thou, Simon Barjona: for flesh and blood hath not revealed it unto thee, but my Father which is in heaven." ¶

(Matt. 16:13-17.) By that same power we know other than by hearsay that this work is divine, and that is my witness to you this day in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ. Amen. ¶

Minutes of the October 1859 General Conference

Conference record · 1859 October General Conference · 1859-10-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News, October 12, 1859. Conference ran four days, October 6-9, 1859, with morning and afternoon/evening sessions each day. All discourses reported by G. D. Watt; published in Journal of Discourses, Volume 7. The seven Brigham Young discourses (Blessings of Association; Possession of the Spirit; Eternal Life; Union; Progress in Knowledge; Reorganization of the High Council; Intelligence) and the discourses by Orson Hyde, Heber C. Kimball, Daniel H. Wells, and John Taylor are already individually transcribed as separate talk files in this vault. Remarks by Joseph Young, Lorenzo Snow, Albert Carrington, Charles C. Rich, Erastus Snow, and E. D. Woolley are referenced in the minutes but have no known individual JD publication and remain untranscribed.

Corrected OCR misreads verbatim against the witness: "Pligh-Priests"->"High-Priests", "sis the Priesthood"->"is the Priesthood", "farreaching"->"far-reaching" (rejoined line-break hyphenation), "Patriarch,with"->"Patriarch, with" (dropped space), "asassembled"->"assembled" (duplicated line-break fragment), "with with Jehovah"->"with Jehovah", "annointed"->"anointed", "despensation"->"dispensation", "fight him in in vain"->"fight him in vain" (duplicated word), the stray mid-phrase period in "died as a . martyr"->"died as a martyr" and in "glorious .work"->"glorious work", "un til finally"->"until finally" (rejoined line-break split), "Prophet of God. and"->"Prophet of God, and" (period/comma misread), "ChurchMay"->"Church — May" (dropped dash at a line-break, restored as an em dash to mark the clause break, matching the printed page's own hyphen-at-linebreak artifact), "God,that"->"God, that" (dropped space), and "bessings"->"blessings". Left as printed per the restraint principle: the stray apostrophes in "Patriarchs,'", "'such as", "him'", and "mem'ry" (a plausible period-typical elided hymn spelling), and the stray footnote-like "1" in "wards and,1 the" — all ambiguous printer/OCR artifacts in the witness with no single unambiguous resolution. This completes the review of the first Clawson talk of this conference (distinct from the later, shorter "stability of Church" address).

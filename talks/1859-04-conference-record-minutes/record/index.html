Minutes of the April 1859 General Conference

Conference record · 1859 April General Conference · 1859-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes (April 13, 1859) of the April 6-7, 1859 General Conference. Stub summaries for Joseph Young, Heber C. Kimball, George A. Smith, John Young, Daniel H. Wells (April 6), and John Taylor, Brigham Young (April 7) are all filed separately; confirmed during heavyweight review that none of these discourses were published in the Journal of Discourses (no JD Vol. 7 entries exist for this conference). Corrected during heavyweight review; a prior pass of this note incorrectly implied a fuller JD transcription pass remained open. Conference adjourned until October 6, 1859.

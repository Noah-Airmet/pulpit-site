Sustaining of the General Authorities and Officers of the Church

Heber J. Grant · 1926 October General Conference · 1926-10-05

Fidelity

◕Authoritative print

Transcribed from the OCR of the official printed Conference Report published by the Deseret News and hosted at the Internet Archive (conferencereport1926sa). Running heads, page numbers, and line-break hyphenation removed. This is the printed record of the sustaining vote (a list of names and callings), not a discourse; formatted to match the paired-name-per-line layout used for the equivalent list in the April 1926 conference (1926-04-52).

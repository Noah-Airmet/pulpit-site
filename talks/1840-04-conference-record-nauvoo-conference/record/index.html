Conference record · 1840 April General Conference · 1840-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Preferred witness is the Joseph Smith Papers featured text of the Times and Seasons minutes, not verbatim sermon transcripts. The record preserves conference minutes, business, resolutions, and a summarized Joseph Smith discourse; discrete complete talks are not recoverable from this source.

Minutes of the April 1862 General Conference

Conference record · 1862 April General Conference · 1862-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes (April 16, 1862, pp. 332-333) of the Thirty-Second Annual Conference, April 6-9, 1862. All fourteen discourses from this conference are filed separately as full talks: Brigham Young's 'Government of the Tongue—Impartiality in Judgment—Sealing,' 'Home Manufactures—Certain Destruction of the Enemies of Truth,' 'Duties Connected With the Aaronic and Melchizedek Priesthood,' 'Authority of Bishops—Branch Organizations—Assisting the Mail and Telegraph Companies,' and 'Forming a State Constitution—Raising Agricultural Products—True Riches'; Orson Hyde's 'Trusting in the Almighty' and 'Bishops and Presidents'; George A. Smith's 'Necessity of Understanding the First Principles of the Gospel,' 'Corruption Among Government Officials,' and 'Counsel on Cattle Herding'; Daniel H. Wells' 'Instructions on Priesthood—Necessity for Diligence Among the Saints'; Heber C. Kimball's 'Necessity of the Saints Possessing the Spirit of Revelation'; Ezra T. Benson's 'Gratitude for the Blessings of the Gospel'; and Wilford Woodruff's 'Necessity of the Living Oracles Among the Saints.' This record covers only the surrounding conference business (attendance, sustaining, session structure, reports, hymns/prayers) and is not itself a discourse.

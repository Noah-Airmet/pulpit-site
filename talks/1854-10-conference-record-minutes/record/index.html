Minutes of the October 1854 General Conference

Conference record · 1854 October General Conference · 1854-10-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the October 6, 1854 General Conference (and following days). All discourses from this conference that survive in the Journal of Discourses are filed separately as full talks: Benson's return-from-the-plains discourse, Young's 'Debtors to the Perpetual Emigrating Fund' and 'Marriage Relations of Bishops and Deacons,' Smith's 'Perpetual Emigrating Fund, Etc.' (JD 7:59-62), Kimball's 'Adherence to Mormonism' (JD 7:39-41), Grant's 'The Immigration' (JD 2:71-74), Pratt's 'The Perpetual Emigrating Fund' (JD 2:54-61), and Hyde's 'Gathering the Saints' (JD 2:61-70) and 'The Marriage Relations' (JD 2:75-87). Corrected during heavyweight review; a prior pass of this note incorrectly claimed all of these except Benson/Young-debtors were not yet transcribed, when they already existed as separate files. Orson Hyde's short morning remarks praising the relief volunteers are summarized here rather than filed separately given their brevity.

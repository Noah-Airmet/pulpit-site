Minutes of the October 1869 General Conference

Conference record · 1869 October General Conference · 1869-10-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the Thirty-Ninth Semi-Annual Conference, October 6-10, 1869 (corrected from a prior 'Fortieth' misnumbering — the Weebly page's own masthead reads 'THIRTY-NINTH SEMI-ANNUAL CONFERENCE'). All 5 discourses with surviving JD full text are filed separately as talks: Orson Hyde's 'The Right to Lead the Church, Etc.' (Wednesday PM, October 6), Orson Pratt's 'Celestial Marriage' (Thursday PM, October 7), George A. Smith's 'Celestial Marriage—Bishops and Deacons Should Be Married—Divorce' (Friday PM, October 8), and George Q. Cannon's 'Celestial Marriage' and Lorenzo Snow's 'Acting in the Name of the Lord' (both Saturday, October 9) — confirmed against the Weebly page's own bibliography, which lists no other JD citations for this conference. Note: the Weebly page's own agenda listing misplaces Snow's discourse under Thursday, October 7, but both the JD scan's own printed dateline and the Weebly bibliography agree on October 9; the agenda entry is treated as the source's own compilation error. The many other named speakers (Wells, Woodruff, Rich, Erastus Snow, Richards, Brigham Young Jr., Joseph F. Smith, Brigham Young's several appearances) are recorded only as third-person Deseret News summaries, with no full JD text located — consistent with the bibliography, not a vault gap.

Minutes of the April 1865 General Conference

Conference record · 1865 April General Conference · 1865-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the Thirty-Fifth Annual Conference, April 6-9, 1865, preserved only as third-person summaries in this source — no verbatim discourse transcripts for this specific conference were located. (The Historical General Conferences page nominally covering April 1865 also carries a full transcript of an Amasa M. Lyman discourse, but that discourse's own dateline reads 'General Conference, Oct. 9, 1865' — it belongs to the October 1865 conference and has been reserved for filing there, not duplicated here.) Filed entirely as a conference_record; if verbatim JD transcripts for this April 1865 conference surface later, individual talks should be split out at that time.

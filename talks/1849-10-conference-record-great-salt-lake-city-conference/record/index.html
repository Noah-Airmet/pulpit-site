Summary of the October 1849 Great Salt Lake City General Conference

Conference record · 1849 October General Conference · 1849-10-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Transcribed from the Millennial Star, Vol. XII, No. 8 (April 15, 1850), pp. 122-124, 'A Summary of the Business Transacted at the General Conference, Held at the Great Salt Lake City, Oct. 6th and 7th, 1849,' a narrative editorial summary rather than a verbatim transcript, though it does quote a few short passages in direct speech. A heavyweight pass located a CHL manuscript witness ('Minutes of the General Conference, held Great Salt Lake City, Oct. 6 & 7, 1849,' a copy by Thomas Bullock, 8 leaves), which gives a fuller named attendee list than the Millennial Star summary and confirms the summary's account of the Perpetual Emigrating Fund and missionary-appointment business, including naming the PEF committee members. That manuscript itself notes 'See following for original minutes with more detail,' pointing to a second, fuller CHL item not yet transcribed. Graded fragmentary per schema.md's rule that conference_record entries are never verbatim, and doubly so here since most of the body is still the Millennial Star's summary rather than the manuscript's own text.

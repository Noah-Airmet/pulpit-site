Man, the Offspring of God, a Dual Being Immediate Revelation Operate With the Priesthood

John Taylor · 1875 April General Conference · 1875-04-08

Fidelity

◑Contemporaneous report

Published in Journal of Discourses, Volume 17, pp. 369-376, continued on Volume 18, pp. 1-2 (this discourse crosses a JD volume boundary; the printed text itself is continuous). Reported by David W. Evans in Pitman shorthand; the speaker did not review this text before publication. Text transcribed from the printed JD, cross-checked against the Weebly raw-HTML transcript for the Volume 18 continuation (that page's IA/OCR scan is severely garbled two-column text).

Deseret Currency and Self-Sufficiency

Daniel H. Wells, Second Counselor in the First Presidency

1858 April General Conference·Afternoon Session·1858-04-06

Brief remarks by President Daniel H. Wells at the April 1858 General Conference. Reported in conference minutes. ¶

Wells spoke on circulating medium and the importance of self-sufficiency through production of needed goods, and urged the removal of wickedness from the community. ¶

Minutes of the April 1848 Log Tabernacle General Conference, Council Bluffs/Kanesville, Iowa

Conference record · 1848 April General Conference · 1848-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Full transcription pass (2026-07-24, claude-fable-5): all 22 leaves of the CHL manuscript (Historian's Office general Church minutes, 1839-1877, '1848 April 6-9' / 'General Conference minutes, Iowa,' CR 100 318, recorded by Thomas Bullock) are now transcribed below by direct reading of the CHL leaf images, fetched via the catalog's own delivery API (accessLevelCode: unconditional confirmed per leaf before download). Method: each leaf was read as four overlapping 2x-scale contrast-enhanced bands, with additional 3-4x zoom crops for hard words. The record covers all four days (April 6-9, 1848): organization, the sustaining of the reorganized First Presidency and church officers, discourses by Hyde, the Youngs, Kimball, Pratt, Woodruff, G. A. Smith, Morley, Emer Harris, and others, the team-raising business, the naming of Kanesville, and adjournment to October 6. Still graded fragmentary, not because content is missing, but because Bullock's fast longhand carries real word-level uncertainty; every uncertain reading is bracketed in place ([word?], [illegible]) rather than smoothed over, cancellations are recorded as [struck: ...], and interlineations as ^[...]. A second reader collating against the same images would tighten specific bracketed spots.

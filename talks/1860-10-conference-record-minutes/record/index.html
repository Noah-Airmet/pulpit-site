Minutes of the October 1860 General Conference

Conference record · 1860 October General Conference · 1860-10-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes (October 10, 1860, p. 253) of the October 6-8, 1860 Semi-Annual Conference. All nine discourses from this conference are filed separately as full talks: Heber C. Kimball's 'The Latter-day Kingdom' (JD 8:247-253), George A. Smith's 'Testimony' (JD 8:253-255), Brigham Young's 'Testimony of the Spirit—Counsel to the Bishops' (JD 8:315-318) and 'Helping in the Immigration' (JD 8:192-193), Orson Hyde's 'Testimonies of the Truth' (JD 8:232-238), Young's 'Persecution—The Kingdom of God' (JD 8:194-200) and 'Joys of Eternity' (JD 8:200), Daniel H. Wells' 'Duties of the Saints' (JD 9:118-120), and Young's 'Funds of the Church' (JD 8:201-205). This record file covers only the surrounding conference business (roster, sustainings, financial reports, minutes synopsis).

Blessings Secured By Faithfulness

Heber C. Kimball · 1866 April General Conference · 1866-04-04

Fidelity

◑Contemporaneous report

Delivered April 4, 1866, two days before the formal April 6 conference opening (this conference's preliminary sessions began April 4). Published in Journal of Discourses, Volume 11, pp. 208-212 (confirmed against indexes/jod-page-index.json and the Weebly bibliography during heavyweight review). Text below is taken from the Historical General Conferences transcript. Pitman shorthand report by George D. Watt; speaker did not review this text before publication. Full text reproduced; not abridged. Contains period doctrinal teaching defending plural marriage, reproduced verbatim per the project's no-alteration rule, not as an editorial endorsement.

Minutes of the April 1876 General Conference

Conference record · 1876 April General Conference · 1876-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the April 6-9, 1876 General Conference (Forty-Sixth Annual Conference). First Presidency: Brigham Young, Daniel H. Wells. All 4 discourses with surviving JD full text are filed separately as talks: Wilford Woodruff's 'Infidelity of the Christian World...' and Daniel H. Wells' 'Unbelief of the Present Age...' (Thursday April 6 AM); John Taylor's 'How God's Purposes Are Fulfilled...' (Thursday PM); and Joseph F. Smith's 'The Gospel Trumpet...' (Friday April 7 PM per the Weebly agenda, though this file carries an April 8 date) — confirmed against the Weebly page's own bibliography, which lists no other JD citations for this conference. The many other named speakers (Snow, Rich, Richards, Bullock, Spencer, Penrose, Pratt, Little, Angus M. Cannon, Burton, MacDonald, Caine, Brigham Young) are recorded only as third-person Deseret News summaries, with no full JD text located — consistent with the bibliography, not a vault gap.

Immortality and Eternal Life

Milton R. Hunter · 1953 April General Conference · 1953-04-05

Fidelity

◕Authoritative print

Official printed text of the One Hundred Twenty-third Annual (April) General Conference, held April 4, 5, and 6, 1953. The Church History Catalog record for the April 1953 Conference Report could not be independently verified this session (client-rendered catalog site; no browser access available); the Internet Archive scan (conferencereport1953a) is retained as the top-level source rather than guessing a record ID. Heavyweight review collated the text page-by-page against that scan (leaves 76-80); the opening drop-cap word "TODAY" had been OCR-mangled into the garbled title/id "Ftoday"; corrected the body's opening word to "Today" (the id/filename is kept immutable per schema even though the source of the typo is now understood). Separated numerous scripture block-quotes (Moses 1:39, Matt. 28:5-6, D&C 29:43-44, Alma 11:44,41, D&C 6:7/11:7/14:7, D&C 131:1-4, D&C 132:16-17,19,20-22,24, D&C 84:38,43-44, 2 Nephi 31:17-21, D&C 19:17-18/Mosiah 3:7/Luke 22:44, Mosiah 5:6-10, D&C 4:5/82:19) from surrounding prose that OCR had merged together.

Minutes of the October 1865 General Conference

Conference record · 1865 October General Conference · 1865-10-06

Deseret News minutes of the Semi-Annual Conference, October 6-9, 1865. All ten discourses given during this conference are filed separately as full talks: [[1865-10-kimball-others-sins-no-justification-of-ours]], [[1865-10-hyde-instructions-concerning-things-temporal-and-spiritual]], [[1865-10-smith-prosperity-of-the-saints]], [[1865-10-taylor-revelation-from-god-true-knowledge]], [[1865-10-cannon-riches-of-the-gospel]], [[1865-10-smith-synopsis-of-remarks]], [[1865-10-wells-eternal-life-revealed-in-the-gospel]], [[1865-10-lyman-development-of-the-understanding-necessary]], [[1865-10-benson-exhortation-to-home-manufacture]], and [[1865-10-young-home-manufacturing-merchandising-and-general-economy]]. The closing dismissal by Patriarch John Smith is preserved in full below.

Session notes: Second Day convened, B.Y.U. choir and prayer

Conference record

1912 April General Conference·Second Day Morning Session·1912-04-06

In the Tabernacle, Saturday, April 6, 10 a.m. ¶

Conference was called to order by President Joseph F. Smith. ¶

The Brigham Young University choir of Provo, and soloists, under the direction of Prof. Anthon C. Lund, with Prof. Claire Reid at the organ, rendered the musical numbers at the morning and afternoon sessions of the conference this day. ¶

The B.Y.U. choir sang the hymn "O Awake, My Slumbering Minstrel." ¶

Prayer was offered by Elder Hugh J. Cannon. ¶

The choir sang an anthem, "The Earth Is the Lord's." ¶

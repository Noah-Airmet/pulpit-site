Minutes of the April 1871 General Conference

Conference record · 1871 April General Conference · 1871-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News, April 12, 1871, minutes of the April 6-8, 1871 General Conference (41st Annual Conference). Reported by David W. Evans; transcribed by Feramorz Young and John Q. Cannon. Source: Journal of Discourses, Volume 14. George Q. Cannon's, Orson Pratt's, and Brigham Young's two discourses are transcribed as separate talk files. JD discourse texts for George A. Smith, Orson Hyde, Lorenzo Snow, John Taylor, and Joseph F. Smith are NOT yet individually transcribed — strong candidate for a follow-up pass.

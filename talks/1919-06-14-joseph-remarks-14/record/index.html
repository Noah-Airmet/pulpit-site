Heavyweight pass: corrected speaker/speaker_id from truncated OCR fragment ('Joseph'/'joseph', originally a mis-split running head 'W. McMURRIN. 25') to 'Joseph W. McMurrin'. This file had erroneously bundled two things: (1) the tail end of Rulon S. Wells's preceding address (1919-06-13), which had been cut off mid-sentence there and bled into the top of this file after a running-head page artifact; and (2) McMurrin's own complete, separately-headed address ('JOS. W. McMURRIN (Of the First Council of Seventy.)'). Removed the misplaced Wells material (restored verbatim to 1919-06-13, its correct home) and the redundant speaker-attribution header, leaving only McMurrin's genuine remarks. Also removed a mid-body running-head bleed ('ELDER CHARLES H. HART.') that had split a sentence. Fixed numerous OCR letter errors ('(n my feelings'->'in my feelings', "with whom' T"->'with whom I', 'childen'->'children', 'srospel'->'gospel', a stray '»' glyph, 'obe_dience'->'obedience', 'IJhave'->'I have', "who' ever"->'who ever', 'ail mighty'->'all mighty', a mis-joined page-break hyphenation 'run\n\nning'->'running', '.Israel'->'Israel'). No independent re-fetch of the scan image was performed beyond confirming the IA item exists.

Minutes of the April 1878 General Conference

Conference record · 1878 April General Conference · 1878-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the April 6-8, 1878 General Conference (Forty-Eighth Annual Conference). President John Taylor presiding (Brigham Young died August 29, 1877). Quorum of the Twelve present: John Taylor, Wilford Woodruff, Orson Hyde, Orson Pratt, Charles C. Rich, Lorenzo Snow, Erastus Snow, Franklin D. Richards, George Q. Cannon, Brigham Young Jr., Joseph F. Smith. Counselors: John W. Young and D. H. Wells. All 5 discourses with surviving JD full text are filed separately as talks: Wilford Woodruff's 'The Blessings Realized By the Saints...' (Saturday April 6 AM) and George Q. Cannon's 'Labors and Experience of the Elders...' (Saturday PM, per its own JD citation — the Weebly page's agenda does not explicitly list this discourse under April 6, and the same discourse is oddly also listed on the October 1878 Weebly page's own agenda, an apparent site-compilation mix-up); Orson Pratt's 'Parable of the Vineyard...' and George Q. Cannon's 'Ideas Held By the Latter-day Saints...' (both Sunday April 7 AM); and John Taylor's 'No Man Can Direct the Kingdom of God...' (Sunday PM) — confirmed against the Weebly page's own bibliography, which lists no other JD citations for this conference. The many other named speakers (Lorenzo Snow's separate remarks, Richards, Erastus Snow, Brigham Young Jr., Joseph F. Smith) are recorded only as third-person Deseret News summaries, with no full JD text located — consistent with the bibliography, not a vault gap.

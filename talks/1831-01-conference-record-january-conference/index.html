Historical introduction

The conference convened at Fayette on 1 January 1831 pursuant to the September adjournment. Its surviving record consists only of a notice that a commandment was received; the referenced text is now Doctrine and Covenants 38. ¶

This material remains one conference record because the surviving sources preserve proceedings, retrospective summaries, or reported remarks rather than a set of discrete, complete sermon transcripts. ¶

1 January 1831 • Saturday

Conference convened according to adjournment but there were no minutes taken, save a commandment received, giving directions to the Saints, see Book of Commandments. Page 80. 80 p. 2 ¶

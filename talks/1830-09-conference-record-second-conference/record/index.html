Minutes of the Second Conference of the Church

Conference record · 1830 September General Conference · 1830-09-26

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Preferred witness is contemporaneous conference minutes by Oliver Cowdery in Minute Book 2. The record preserves business, appointments, ordination, remarks, and exhortation, but not full sermon texts. A later History A-1 narrative is included as a secondary witness and gives a retrospective description of conference events.

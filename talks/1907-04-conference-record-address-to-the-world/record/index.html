An Address: The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints to the World

Conference record · 1907 April General Conference · 1907-04-05

Fidelity

◕Authoritative print

This is the official "Address to the World" declaration signed by the First Presidency (John R. Winder and Anthon H. Lund, on behalf of the Presidency) and adopted by vote of the conference on April 5, 1907. It is not a discourse; it was read aloud by Elder Orson F. Whitney during the First Day Morning Session (see 1907-04-smith-congratulations-and-growth.md and 1907-04-winder-endorsement-of-address.md for the surrounding session record) and printed as an appendix to the official Conference Report. Reclassified as record_type: conference_record. Transcribed from the OCR of the official printed Conference Report (conferencereport1907a), collated against the DjVu XML reading-order reconstruction.

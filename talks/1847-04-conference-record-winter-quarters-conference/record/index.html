Minutes of the April 1847 Winter Quarters General Conference

Conference record · 1847 April General Conference · 1847-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Preferred witness is the CHL manuscript for this conference (Historian's Office general Church minutes, 1839-1877, '1847 March-April' / 'General Conference minutes, Winter Quarters,' CR 100 318, 4 leaves, recorded by Thomas Bullock). The four manuscript images were reconstructed from the CHL DZI image tiles and read directly in a follow-up transcription pass. The minutes are fragmentary and difficult: some words are faint, show-through is heavy, and several passages are summarized notes rather than full discourse text. Bracketed readings below preserve uncertainty rather than smoothing the transcript. Graded fragmentary because this is a minutes record and summarized discourse report, not a verbatim transcript.

All four CHL leaf images were reconstructed from DZI tiles and transcribed conservatively; bracketed words mark unresolved readings.

Andrew Jenson, Church Chronology (Salt Lake City: Deseret News, 1899), entry on the Patriarchal successionarchived

OCR'd from churchchronology00jensrich_djvu.txt, lines ~516-521. Jenson states that John Smith (uncle of Joseph Smith, Sen.) 'had been sustained as a "Patriarch in the Church" as early as the general conference, held at Winter Quarters, April 6, 1847.' Retained as a secondary corroborating source now that the CHL manuscript is the preferred witness; the manuscript's John Smith speaking role (per its catalog description) is consistent with this sustaining.

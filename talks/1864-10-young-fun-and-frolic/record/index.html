Fun and Frolic

Brigham Young · 1864 October General Conference · 1864-10-08

Fidelity

◑Contemporaneous report

Delivered October 8, 1864, after the sustaining of the General Authorities. Heavyweight review confirmed this discourse under this title does not appear in the Journal of Discourses: a BYU ContentDM full-text search of the JournalOfDiscourses3 collection for "Fun and Frolic" returns zero results, and the surrounding pages of JD Volume 10 (immediately following Young's October 7 "Necessity of a Living Testimony," JD 10:339-340) proceed directly to George Q. Cannon's October 23, 1864 Tabernacle discourse with no intervening October 8 sermon. It appears this discourse was reported in the Deseret News (per the Historical General Conferences/Weebly transcript, itself sourced from the Deseret News) but was never typeset for the printed JD volumes. Reverted source to the Deseret News/Utah Digital Newspapers as the best-evidenced provenance, with the Weebly transcript as the text-channel witness; the BYU ContentDM collection landing page is retained only as a secondary note that no JD printing was located there, not as a claim of JD provenance. Reporter not specified in the source; speaker did not review this text before publication. Full text reproduced (word-for-word collated against the Weebly witness); not abridged.

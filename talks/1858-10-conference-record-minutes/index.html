1858 October General Conference·Whole Conference·1858-10-06

Minutes of the General Conference of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, Great Salt Lake City, October 1858. President Brigham Young presiding. ¶

No verbatim JD sermon texts have been located for this conference in the sources consulted. The Historical General Conferences (Weebly) page for this conference returned 404 as of June 2026. ¶

Context: The Utah War had ended by June 1858, with President Buchanan extending a general pardon and Alfred Cumming installed as the new civilian governor. Johnston's Army was encamped at Camp Floyd (Cedar Valley), about 40 miles southwest of Salt Lake City. The Saints who had evacuated northward during the "Move South" of spring 1858 had returned to their homes by late summer. This was the first General Conference following the resolution of the Utah War crisis. ¶

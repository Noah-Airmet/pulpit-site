Minutes of the October 1867 General Conference

Conference record · 1867 October General Conference · 1867-10-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the Thirty-Seventh Semi-Annual Conference, October 6-9, 1867 — the first General Conference held in the newly completed Salt Lake Tabernacle. All 5 discourses with surviving JD full text are filed separately as talks: Orson Pratt's 'Temples in Ancient America...' (Monday AM, JD 19), George A. Smith's 'Condition of The Saints in Great Britain' (Monday PM, JD 12), Erastus Snow's 'Life and Health—Matrimony—Education—Home Productions' (Tuesday AM, JD 12), and George A. Smith's 'Education—Phonetics...' and Lorenzo Snow's 'Necessity of Performing the Duties...' (both Wednesday AM, JD 12) — confirmed against the Weebly page's own bibliography, which lists no other JD citations for this conference. The many other named speakers on the Weebly page's agenda (Heber C. Kimball, Daniel H. Wells, Orson Hyde, Brigham Young [multiple], John Taylor, Wilford Woodruff, Ezra T. Benson, Joseph Young, George Q. Cannon, Charles C. Rich, John Van Cott, Joseph F. Smith, Hyrum Dayton) are recorded only as third-person Deseret News summaries, with no full JD text located — consistent with the bibliography, not a vault gap.

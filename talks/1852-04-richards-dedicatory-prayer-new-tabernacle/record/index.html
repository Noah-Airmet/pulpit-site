Dedicatory Prayer of the New Tabernacle

Willard Richards · 1852 April General Conference · 1852-04-06

Fidelity

◑Contemporaneous report

Published in Journal of Discourses, Volume 6, pp. 298-306 (reported April 6, 1852), and in the Deseret News, April 17, 1852, p. 46. Pitman shorthand report by George D. Watt; the speaker did not review this text before publication. Text transcribed from the printed JD; previously abridged, now restored to the full unabridged prayer (see heavyweight_review_notes).

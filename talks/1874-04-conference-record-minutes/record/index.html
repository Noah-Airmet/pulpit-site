Minutes of the April 1874 General Conference

Conference record · 1874 April General Conference · 1874-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

The Historical General Conferences (Weebly) page for this conference does not exist (1874-april.html and variants all return 404, reconfirmed 2026-07-01) — a genuine gap in that site's coverage, not a vault problem. The Deseret News for April/May 1874 (Utah Digital Newspapers) is the primary source for the day-by-day minutes. Source for the discourses themselves: Journal of Discourses, Volume 17. This conference opened April 6, 1874 and adjourned to reconvene May 7-9. All 7 discourses with surviving JD full text are filed separately as talks: Orson Pratt's 'The Kingdom not Organized by Man...' (April 6, JD 17); and, from the May continuation, Brigham Young's 'The United Order...,' George A. Smith's 'Zion to Be Redeemed...,' and John Taylor's 'The Position the Saints Have Occupied...' (May 7); Wilford Woodruff's 'Union is Strength...' and Erastus Snow's 'The United Order of Zion...' (May 8); and George A. Smith's 'The Blessings of Eternal Life...' (May 9, the conference's final discourse) — all 7 confirmed via direct collation against the IA OCR scan of JD Volume 17 (no Weebly bibliography exists to cross-check against). Session/time-of-day breakdown (AM/PM) could not be confirmed for the May sessions without an agenda source; talk order was inferred from the discourses' own sequential page numbers in JD.

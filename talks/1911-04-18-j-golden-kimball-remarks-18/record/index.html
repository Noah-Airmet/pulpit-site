Fixed several OCR letter errors (the)' -> they, 1 assume -> I assume, >men -> men, art -> are, Tt -> It, ror -> for, wont -> won't, ud hither -> up hither). Confirmed boundaries clean: begins with Kimball's own remarks (after a trailing musical note from the prior session), ends at his own Amen. Also corrected date and session, which had been systematically mislabeled First Day (1911-04-06) across roughly twenty 1911-04 files; the DjVu day headers (FIRST DAY at leaf 1, SECOND DAY at leaf 30, THIRD DAY at leaf 61) were used to reassign the correct day/session per file via its source_url leaf.

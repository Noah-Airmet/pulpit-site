Minutes of the April 1863 General Conference

Conference record · 1863 April General Conference · 1863-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes (April 22, 1863, p. 337) of the Thirty-Third Annual Conference, April 6-7, 1863. All eight discourses from this conference are filed separately as full talks: Daniel H. Wells' 'Building the Temple and a New Tabernacle—Labor Tithing—Call for Faithful Laborers' (JD 10:139-143), Heber C. Kimball's 'Building the Temple—Endowments—Counsel to Missionaries, Etc.' (JD 10:163-169), George A. Smith's 'Missionary Fund—Support of the Families of Elders Who Are on Missions' (JD 10:143-145), John Taylor's 'The Blessings the Saints Will Enjoy—How the Kingdom of God is to Be Established—Building Temples, Tabernacles, and Houses—Gathering the Poor' (JD 10:145-151), Ezra T. Benson's 'Practical Duties of the Saints—Blessings Resulting From Their Performance' (JD 10:151-154), Charles C. Rich's 'Building the Temple—General Duties of the Saints' (JD 10:160-163), Orson Hyde's 'Destruction of Babylon—How the Saints Are to Become Saviors of the World' (JD 10:155-159), and Amasa M. Lyman's 'Advice to Missionaries—Preaching the Gospel—Gathering the Poor, Etc.' (JD 10:178-187). This record covers conference business, session order, sustaining of authorities, and remarks not otherwise preserved as separate talks.

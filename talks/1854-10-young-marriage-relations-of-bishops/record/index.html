Marriage Relations of Bishops and Deacons

Brigham Young · 1854 October General Conference · 1854-10-06

Fidelity

◑Contemporaneous report

Published in Journal of Discourses, Volume 2, pp. 88-90 (delivered October 6, 1854, immediately following Orson Hyde's 'The Marriage Relations' lecture). This is Young's second discourse at the October 1854 conference; his first ('Debtors to the Perpetual Emigrating Fund,' JD 2:49-53) is filed separately. Text below is taken from the raw HTML of the historicalgeneralconferences.weebly.com transcript (fetched directly to avoid AI-summarization truncation), cross-checked against jod.mrm.org as a private clue. Pitman shorthand report; the speaker did not review this text before publication. Discusses 1850s Church teaching on plural marriage and bishop/deacon qualifications verbatim per the project's no-alteration rule, not as an editorial endorsement.

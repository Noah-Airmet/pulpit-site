Minutes of the April 1852 General Conference

Conference record · 1852 April General Conference · 1852-04-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the April 6-11, 1852 General Conference (held at the newly dedicated New Tabernacle), covering sustainings and administrative business. This file does not attempt to capture every session: the dedicatory prayer (Willard Richards) and the April 6 morning discourse by Brigham Young ('Weakness and Impotence of Men') are filed separately as full talks, as are three other Young discourses from this conference ('The Lord at the Head of His Kingdom,' JD 6:314-322, April 7; 'Education,' JD 1:66-71, April 8; 'Self-Government...,' JD 1:46-53, April 9) — all four already exist as separate talk files in this vault (corrected during heavyweight review; a prior pass of this note incorrectly claimed they were not yet transcribed). Remarks by Orson Pratt, Orson Spencer, George A. Smith, W. W. Phelps, Heber C. Kimball, and others across April 6-11 are NOT yet transcribed into this vault and remain open work for a future ingestion pass.

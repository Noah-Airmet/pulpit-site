Historical Address

George A. Smith · 1868 October General Conference · 1868-10-08

Fidelity

◑Contemporaneous report

Published in Journal of Discourses, Volume 13, pp. 103-124, as a single combined entry (confirmed against indexes/jod-page-index.json and the Weebly page's own bibliography, which lists only one JD citation dated October 8, 1868). The Weebly page's own agenda lists this as a two-part address — 'History of Church Part 1 from Kirtland to Nauvoo' Wednesday, October 7, 2 p.m., and 'History of Church Part 2 Emigration to Utah' Thursday, October 8, 10 a.m. — while the printed JD's own table of contents dates it 'Oct. 8, 9, 1868.' JD published the two parts as one continuous 22-page discourse; this file reproduces that combined JD text in full, verified against the IA scan (opening 'ing of no ordinary character. In all the Conferences...' through closing '...cease to fellowship every man that will not build up Zion. Amen.,' confirmed verbatim, ~2600 OCR lines, no truncation). Reported by David W. Evans in Pitman shorthand; the speaker did not review this text before publication. Text transcribed from the printed JD.

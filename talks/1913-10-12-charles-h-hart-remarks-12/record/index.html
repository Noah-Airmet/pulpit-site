Collated word-by-word against the original scan.

Heavyweight pass: verified against archive.org/details/conferencereport1913sa leaves 41-45 (page/n40 through page/n44), page-by-page collation against the scan/OCR witness under the "ELDER CHARLES H. HART." running head. Confirmed boundary: opens directly after "The Pioneer" (a soprano solo sung by Sister Edna Anderson, session music, correctly excluded) and closes with "...I pray in the name of Jesus. The running head reading "ELDER LEVI EDGAR YOUNG." atop leaf 44 (printed page 43), while this talk's text continues underneath it, is a page-proof artifact (the running head was evidently set for the page break based on where the NEXT talk begins) and does not indicate a boundary here; it has been removed as running-head material rather than treated as a mid-talk speaker change.

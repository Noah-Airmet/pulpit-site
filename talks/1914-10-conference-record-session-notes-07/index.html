Session notes: hymn and prayer

Conference record

1914 October General Conference·First Day Afternoon Session·1914-10-04

Ye simple souls who stray Far from the path of peace, ¶

That lonely, unfrequented way To life and happiness. ¶

Prayer was offered by Elder Solomon Hale. ¶

The anthem, "Grant us Peace," was sung by the choir, Mrs. Sarah L. Wood and James Moncar rendering the duet. ¶

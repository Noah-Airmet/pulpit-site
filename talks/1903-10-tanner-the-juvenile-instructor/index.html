The Juvenile Instructor

Joseph M. Tanner, Assistant General Superintendent of the Deseret Sunday School Union

1903 October General Conference·Deseret Sunday School Union General Meeting·1903-10-05

In addition to the remarks of Brother Reynolds, I desire to call your attention tonight to the value of the present volume of the Juvenile Instructor to ¶

all Sunday school teachers, and recommend that all of you get the volume for this year bound, as it will be of great help to you in your work. ¶

Minutes and Discourse of the October 1841 Nauvoo General Conference

Conference record · 1841 October General Conference · 1841-10-01

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Preferred witness is the Joseph Smith Papers featured text of the Times and Seasons minutes, not verbatim sermon transcripts. The record preserves conference minutes, business actions, and a substantial reported summary of Joseph Smith's discourse on baptism for the dead; discrete complete talks are not recoverable from this source.

Rudger Clawson · 1898 October General Conference · 1898-10-09

◕Authoritative print

Transcribed from the OCR of the official printed Conference Report of the Sixty-ninth Semi-Annual Conference (October 1898), scanned and hosted at the Internet Archive (conferencereport1898sa). OCR was cleaned and corrected: running heads, page numbers, and line-break hyphenation removed; obvious OCR errors fixed. Not yet proofread page-by-page against the scan images, so minor residual OCR errors may remain (status: draft, pending review).

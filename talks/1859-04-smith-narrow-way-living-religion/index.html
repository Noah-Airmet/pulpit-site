The Narrow Way and Living One's Religion

George A. Smith, Apostle, Quorum of the Twelve

1859 April General Conference·Wednesday Afternoon Session·1859-04-06

Remarks by Elder George A. Smith at the April 1859 General Conference (Wednesday afternoon session, April 6). Reported in conference minutes. ¶

Smith cited Matthew 7:14 ("Straight is the gate and narrow is the way") and reasoned on the purification of the Saints and the difficulty of getting men to truly live their religion. ¶

Dedicatory Prayer—Northwest Cornerstone, Salt Lake Temple

George B. Wallace · 1853 April General Conference · 1853-04-06

Fidelity

◑Contemporaneous report

Delivered April 6, 1853, at the Northwest cornerstone of the Salt Lake Temple. George B. Wallace offered the dedicatory prayer over the NW cornerstone. This prayer was part of the same four-cornerstone ceremony that produced Young's SE oration, Kimball's SE prayer (filed separately), Hunter's SW oration, Cordon's SW prayer (filed separately), and John Young's NW oration (filed separately). Published in Journal of Discourses, Volume 2, pp. 41-42 (confirmed via the weebly bibliography and cross-checked against jod.mrm.org). Pitman shorthand report by George D. Watt; the speaker did not review this text before publication.

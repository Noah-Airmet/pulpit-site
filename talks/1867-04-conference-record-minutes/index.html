Minutes of the April 1867 General Conference

Conference record

1867 April General Conference·Whole Conference·1867-04-06

The Thirty-Seventh Annual Conference of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, convened in the Tabernacle, Great Salt Lake City, April 6-8, 1867, President Brigham Young presiding. ¶

Saturday, April 6 Morning Session: President Brigham Young set a "mark" for the Elders to preach to — the unity of the Saints — and delivered his [[1867-04-young-the-elders-to-labor-for-the-unity-of-the-saints]] discourse (filed separately). President Heber C. Kimball spoke on the necessity of the Spirit of God, self-sufficiency, and observing the Word of Wisdom (summary only; never printed in full in JD). President Young then delivered [[1867-04-young-how-the-sisters-can-help-to-build-up-the-kingdom]] (filed separately), urging the sisters to economize on tea, coffee, and hired help and devote the savings to the Perpetual Emigrating Fund and missionary support. ¶

Saturday, April 6 Afternoon Session: Elder John Taylor delivered an extensive discourse, [[1867-04-taylor-political-and-social-economy]] (filed separately), on the comprehensive scope of the Saints' religion in both temporal and spiritual affairs. President Daniel H. Wells then delivered a separate discourse, "The Kingdom of God Will Endure Forever" — reported only in The Deseret News (May 1, 1867, pp. 138-139) per the Weebly bibliography, not in JD, and not yet transcribed as a separate talk in this vault. ¶

Sunday, April 7 Morning Session: Elder George A. Smith delivered [[1867-04-smith-raising-flax-and-woolhome-manufactureschurch-literaturefolly]] and President Brigham Young delivered [[1867-04-young-the-word-of-wisdom]] (both filed separately). ¶

Sunday, April 7 Afternoon Session: Elder Wilford Woodruff delivered [[1867-04-woodruff-necessity-of-unity-in-faith-and]], Elder Ezra T. Benson delivered [[1867-04-benson-word-of-wisdomhappiness-to-be-obtained]], Elder George Q. Cannon delivered [[1867-04-cannon-necessity-of-union-and-obedience-to]], and President Brigham Young delivered [[1867-04-young-every-saint-on-a-mission]] (all filed separately). ¶

Monday, April 8 Morning Session: President Heber C. Kimball spoke on temperate living and urged pushing forward the building of the Temple (summary only; never printed in full in JD). President Brigham Young then delivered [[1867-04-young-building-the-templemormonism-embraces-all-truth]] (filed separately). ¶

Monday, April 8 Afternoon Session: Elder Charles C. Rich delivered [[1867-04-rich-labor-to-build-up-the-kingdom]] (filed separately). The General Authorities of the Church were sustained by vote of the Conference. President Daniel H. Wells then delivered [[1867-04-wells-schools-and-school-teacherstithing-etc]], and President Brigham Young delivered the closing address, [[1867-04-young-educationemployment-of-females]] (both filed separately), bringing the conference to a close. ¶

George F. Richards · 1913 April General Conference · 1913-04-06

Confirmed boundaries: begins immediately after Andrew Jenson's address with the printed synopsis 'Obedience a heavenly and eternal principle.—Essentials to salvation.—The Redeemer a perfect exemplar of obedience.—Duties to be learned, and diligently fulfilled.' then 'My brethren and sisters, I suppose this congregation is made up in large proportions of members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints...'; ends '...I pray, in the name of Jesus. Amen.', followed by the choir hymn 'Here, by Babylon's way' and benediction by Elder John N. Pike, closing the (First) Overflow Meeting. This is the last address of the Third Day Morning Overflow Meeting; the Second Overflow Meeting (2 p.m., presided over by Elder Joseph F. Smith Jr.) follows as a new session with J. Golden Kimball's address.

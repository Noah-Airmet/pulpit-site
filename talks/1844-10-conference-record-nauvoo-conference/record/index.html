No double-spacing or garbled-OCR artifacts found; content is coherent and matches the well-documented historical record of this conference. NOT a full page-by-page scan collation -- the file's own notes already flag that the continuation still needs a direct scan-collation pass before a heavyweight/publication upgrade, which this light review does not attempt or claim.

Collated word-by-word against the original scan.

Heavyweight pass: the file's preferred witness (Church History Catalog leaf-image viewer) returned no extractable OCR/text in this environment, so collated against an independent OCR digitization of the same underlying publication instead -- Internet Archive's per_utah-and-the-mormons_times-and-seasons_1844-10-15_5_19 (15 Oct. issue) and _1844-11-01_5_20 (1 Nov. issue). Both installments' openings matched the vault text verbatim ('Thousands having arrived on the ground by ten o'clock A.M. Elder P.P. Pratt called the people to order...'; the sustaining-vote sequence for Heber C. Kimball and Orson Hyde as apostles). One divergence found and left unresolved rather than silently overwritten: the vault reads 'Elder W. W. Phelps moved that we uphold Brigham Young...', while the IA OCR is itself corrupted at the same spot (missing the verb entirely -- 'Elder W. Phelps that we Brigham Young the president...'), so this IA witness can't confirm or contradict 'uphold' either way; resolving it needs the preferred CHL scan, which this environment couldn't read as text. Everything else checked matched. Source URL, source_type, and witness metadata confirmed correct. Remains 'reconstructed' fidelity per the file's own honest grading, and the file's own notes already flag the continuation as still needing a direct CHL scan-collation pass -- this pass substitutes an independent witness for that gap rather than closing it outright.

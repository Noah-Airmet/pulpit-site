Joseph Fielding Smith · 1953 April General Conference · 1953-04-04

Fidelity

◕Authoritative print

Official printed text of the One Hundred Twenty-third Annual (April) General Conference, held April 4, 5, and 6, 1953, as published in the Church Conference Report. The Church History Catalog record for the April 1953 Conference Report could not be independently verified this session (the catalog's record viewer is a client-rendered SPA that returned no content to available fetch tools, and the Chrome browser extension was not connected); rather than guess or reuse the 1951-1952 parent record without proof, the Internet Archive scan (conferencereport1953a), a Church History Library-contributed preservation copy, is retained as the top-level source. Heavyweight review collated the text page-by-page against that scan; removed a running-head bleed ("AS E. McKAY 21") from the body, corrected an OCR misread of "Jun." as "Tun." in the Doctrine and Covenants quotation, and rejoined a paragraph the OCR had split mid-sentence.

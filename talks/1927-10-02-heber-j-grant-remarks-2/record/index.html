Heavyweight pass: this was actually one continuous address by President Grant, wrongly split at ingestion into three files (1927-10-02, -03, -04); the middle file was even misattributed to 'Christopherson,' a mission president Grant merely mentions in passing ('Brother Martin Christopherson was released...'), not an actual speaker. Merged all three back into one talk and collated word-for-word against the conferencereport1927sa djvu OCR witness (First Day Morning Meeting, Friday, October 7, 1927). Corrected date from approximate to exact. Removed three bled-in running-head/page-number fragments ('PRESIDENT HEBER J. GRANT' with page numbers 3, 5, and a split word 'sug-GENERAL CONFERENCE-gestion'). Fixed OCR typos: 'Whiat I the Lord' -> 'What I the Lord'; 'vpice' -> 'voice'; 'it js to reign' -> 'it is to reign'; 'makingcrime' -> 'making crime'; 'jail evil' -> 'all evil'; 'Latter-dayv Saint' -> 'Latter-day Saint'; 'tohacco' -> 'tobacco'; a stray apostrophe in "'upon which it is predicated" removed. Confirmed boundary: begins after the opening hymns/prayer and ends with 'Amen.'

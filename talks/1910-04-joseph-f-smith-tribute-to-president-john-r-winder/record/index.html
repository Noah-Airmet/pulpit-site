A Tribute to President John R. Winder

Joseph F. Smith · 1910 April General Conference · 1910-04-03

Fidelity

◕Authoritative print

Transcribed from the OCR of the official printed Conference Report of the Eightieth Annual Conference (April 3, 4, and 6, 1910), published by the Deseret News and hosted at the Internet Archive (conferencereport1910a). Reading order was reconstructed from the DjVu word coordinates with the repository reading-order helper, then cleaned and corrected against the printed text: running heads ("GENERAL CONFERENCE" and the speaker's name), page numbers, and line-break hyphenation removed; obvious OCR errors fixed. The printed report supplies an editorial topic-summary line for each discourse, which is not included in the transcript body; the title here is supplied from that summary and the discourse's central themes. Not yet proofread line-by-line against the page images, so minor residual OCR errors may remain (status: draft, pending review). source_archived_url points to the Internet Archive item itself.

Can Any Of You Remember...

Mark Peterson · 1968 October General Conference · 1968-10-05

Fidelity

◕Authoritative print

Transcribed from the official printed Conference Report of the One Hundred Thirty-eighth Semiannual (October 1968) General Conference, digitized by the Church History Library and hosted at the Internet Archive (item conferencereport1968sa), pp. 95-96. Reading order rebuilt from the item's DjVu OCR word coordinates (scripts/ingest/cr-reading-order.py). This speaker is a lay Aaronic Priesthood teacher, son of Edgar and Ethelyn Peterson of the Las Vegas 27th Ward -- distinct from Elder Mark E. Petersen of the Council of the Twelve, who spoke later in this same session. The back-of-book index lists him as "Peterson, Mark," which the prior heavyweight review flagged as ambiguous and unmatched to any file; this pass resolves the ambiguity and ingests his address (one of three short talks by young Aaronic Priesthood holders that President Alvin R. Dyer introduced together; the other two are Dennis Spackman's and Jeffery Smith's, also newly ingested this pass). Running heads, page numbers, and leaf markers removed; de-hyphenated line breaks and reflowed paragraphs; corrected an isolated OCR character error ("reminc led" for "reminded") without altering wording.

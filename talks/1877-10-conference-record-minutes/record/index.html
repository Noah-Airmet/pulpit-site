Minutes of the October 1877 General Conference

Conference record · 1877 October General Conference · 1877-10-06

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the October 6-7, 1877 General Conference (Forty-Eighth Semi-Annual Conference). This is the first conference following the death of Brigham Young (died August 29, 1877). President John Taylor presided as president of the Quorum of the Twelve. Both discourses with surviving JD full text are filed separately as talks: John Taylor's 'The Voice of God, the Voice of the People...' (Saturday October 6 PM) and 'The Trusteeship—President Young's Labors...' (Sunday October 7 PM) — confirmed against the Weebly page's own bibliography, which lists no other JD citations for this conference. George Q. Cannon's 'An Important Conference—The Death of Joseph and the Death of Brigham...' (Sunday October 7 AM, JD 19:230-237) is likewise cited in the Weebly bibliography but has not been ingested as a separate talk in this vault — a genuine gap, flagged for a future ingestion pass rather than addressed here (out of scope for a heavyweight review of existing files). Woodruff, Erastus Snow (cited only to the Deseret News, not JD), and Franklin D. Richards are recorded only as third-person Deseret News summaries.

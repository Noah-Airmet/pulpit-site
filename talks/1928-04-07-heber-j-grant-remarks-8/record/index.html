Heavyweight pass: collated word-for-word against the conferencereport1928a djvu OCR witness (First Day Afternoon Meeting, Friday, April 6, 1928; leaves 25-30). Corrected speaker attribution: this was misattributed to Heber J. Grant, but the body is almost entirely a guest address by Dr. Roy O. Wyland of the Boy Scouts of America, whom President Grant briefly introduces in the opening paragraphs (retained as part of this file, matching how introductions are handled elsewhere in this vault). Removed three bled-in running-head fragments ('DR. ROY O. IVY LAND' / 'DR. ROY 0. WYLAND', OCR mis-splits of 'WYLAND') left mid-sentence. Fixed OCR typos: 'belongto' -> 'belong to'; 'T cannot see' -> 'I cannot see'; 'con finer!' -> 'confined'; 'Roy Scout' -> 'Boy Scout' (misread B as R). Corrected session/date: this is the First Day Afternoon Meeting, not the Morning Meeting. Confirmed boundary: begins after the afternoon's opening hymn/prayer/quartet and ends with Wyland's closing words on Scouting and the Church.

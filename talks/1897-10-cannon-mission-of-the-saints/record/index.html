Heavyweight pass: confirmed via IA djvu OCR (conferencereport1897sa) that this file and the now-deleted 1897-10-cannon-repentance-and-confession.md were a single continuous discourse artificially split by an earlier ingestion pass, which mistook a mid-sentence running head ('PRESIDENT GEORGE Q. CANNON. 69') for a new talk boundary -- the two files' text joins mid-sentence ('...you are all' / 'right again. Do not delude yourselves...'). Merged the two into this one file, covering the whole printed subject list (Mission of the Saints -- How Satan will be bound -- Approaching Judgments -- Wickedness of the world -- Cleansing process in progress -- Who are the children of God? -- The Spirit of Revelation with the Church -- Return of the Ten Tribes) through its genuine closing 'Amen'. Removed a leaked fragment of the printed subject-list ('world -- Cleansing process...') that had bled into the start of this file's body as if it were prose. A further 'CLOSING REMARKS' fragment that the old repentance-and-confession.md had wrongly appended after Cannon's Amen was Wilford Woodruff's separate closing discourse, not Cannon's -- moved to 1897-10-woodruff-closing-address.md. talk_order renumbered from 5/6 to 3 within Third Day Afternoon Session after upstream reshuffling (see conference notes). Fixed OCR errors (Avhen->when, righteouness->righteousness, antedeluvian->antediluvian, daring->during, or His prophets->of His prophets). 0 QC ERRORs.

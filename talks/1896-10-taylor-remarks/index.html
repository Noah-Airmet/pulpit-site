Remarks

John W. Taylor, Quorum of the Twelve Apostles

1896 October General Conference·Tuesday Morning Session·1896-10-06

[Summary from Deseret Weekly proceedings: Elder John W. Taylor spoke of the control of parents over children, making application of the parable of the wise and foolish virgins. His travels in the outlying stakes had convinced him of the neglect of many youth in performing their religious labors. He commended the work done in the Uintah Stake in taking up a personal labor with the youth. Marriages with those not of the faith were deprecated. He testified to the truths of the Gospel and the authority of the leaders of the Church.] ¶

Telegram to the Conference

Heber J. Grant, President of the Church

1932 October General Conference·Third Day Morning Session·1932-10-09

"Sincerely trust you have had wonderful Conference, as I know you have. We will participate with you first half hour this morning. Ask Professor Lund to have opening hymn this morning 'An angel from on high,' next 'Let the mountains shout for joy.' Again our love and greetings to the Saints and my blessings upon the people everywhere. ¶

Heber J. Grant." ¶

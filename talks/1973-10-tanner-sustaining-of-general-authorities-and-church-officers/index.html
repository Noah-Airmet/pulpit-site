Sustaining of General Authorities and Church Officers

N. Eldon Tanner

1973 October General Conference·Saturday Afternoon Session·1973-10-01

President N. Eldon Tanner ¶

As President Lee has said, we will ask for your sustaining vote or otherwise. ¶

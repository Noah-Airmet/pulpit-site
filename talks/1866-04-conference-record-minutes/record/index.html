Minutes of the April 1866 General Conference

Conference record · 1866 April General Conference · 1866-04-04

Fidelity

○Fragmentary

Deseret News minutes of the Thirty-Sixth Annual Conference, preliminary sessions beginning April 4 and the formal conference April 6-8, 1866. Heber C. Kimball's [[1866-04-kimball-blessings-secured-by-faithfulness]] and John Taylor's [[1866-04-taylor-our-religion-is-from-god]] discourses are filed separately as full talks. Amasa M. Lyman's 'Marriage: Its Benefits' (April 5, JD 11:198-208) survives in full in the source but is NOT yet transcribed as a separate talk in this vault — open for a future ingestion pass. Other remarks (Cannon, Snow, Smith, Rich, Erastus Snow, Hyde, Wells, Benson, Joseph Young) are third-person summaries only, including the April 8 'Plural Marriage and Following the Laws of God' session, which the source likewise preserves only as a summary, not a full transcript.

Giving Heed to the Living Oracles

Joseph Eckersley · 1913 April General Conference · 1913-04-06

Fidelity

◕Authoritative print

Transcribed from the OCR of the official printed Conference Report published by the Deseret News and hosted at the Internet Archive, dedicated April 1913 scan (conferencereport1913a, file prefix conferencereport831chur). Running heads, page numbers, and line-break hyphenation removed. A stretch of this leaf's OCR (concerning individual liberty and freedom from priestcraft) shows heavier letter-substitution corruption than the rest of the scan (e.g. 'onnosite' for 'opposite', 'oriesthood' for 'priesthood', 'nnre' for 'pure'); corrected by evident sense, consistent with docs/review.md's OCR-fix guidance, not reconstructed from memory.

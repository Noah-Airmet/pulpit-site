Session notes: President Smith's introduction of Elder McKay

Conference record

1912 October General Conference·Closing Session·1912-10-06

I would like to say for the information perhaps of a great many of the Latter-day Saints who are present here today, that the last speaker is the great-grandson of the Patriarch Hyrum Smith, and who now holds the position of Presiding Patriarch in the Church. ¶

Minutes and Discourses of the April 1844 Nauvoo General Conference

Conference record · 1844 April General Conference · 1844-04-05

Fidelity

◔Reconstructed

Preferred witness is the Joseph Smith Papers transcript of JS History, volume E-1. The conference record is complete but physically discontinuous: the main record reaches a canceled/displaced King Follett trace at p. 1959, blank/canceled pages follow, and the full adopted King Follett funeral sermon appears later at pp. 1968-1979 before the April 8-9 proceedings resume at p. 1981. This file documents both physical source order and adopted reading order and should not be treated as verbatim transcripts of all discrete talks.

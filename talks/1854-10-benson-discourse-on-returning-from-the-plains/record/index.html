Discourse on Returning from the Plains and the Spirit of the Gathering

Ezra T. Benson · 1854 October General Conference · 1854-10-06

Fidelity

◑Contemporaneous report

Published in the Deseret News, October 19, 1854, p. 116, delivered October 6, 1854, after Benson's return from leading relief efforts for the season's emigration trains on the plains. Title is supplied; the Deseret News heading is simply 'Discourse.' Reported by George D. Watt; speaker did not review before publication.
